Welcome to the Ghanaian birth-name generator.

Frequently, children are given a "day name" which corresponds to the day in the week when they were born. These day names have further meanings concerning the soul and character of the person.
This app, we aim to generate your birth name if you were born in Ghana or from Ghanaian descendant, all you need to input is your birth day and gender.
